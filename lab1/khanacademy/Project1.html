Why Almaty?

Almaty is a great travel destination because it uniquely blends stunning natural beauty (Tian Shan mountains, Big Almaty Lake) with vibrant city culture (Soviet architecture, Silk Road history, bustling bazaars like Green Bazaar), offering outdoor adventures (skiing at Shymbulak, hiking) and rich cultural experiences (Zenkov Cathedral, museums, local cuisine) in an accessible, affordable, and increasingly popular Central Asian hub

What you will see

The Almaty Mountains, part of the Northern Tian Shan range, are a stunning natural backdrop to the city of Almaty, Kazakhstan.

Almaty's streets are generally praised for being green, clean,
and walkable, offering a mix of Soviet-era grandeur and modern vibrancy, with popular pedestrian zones like Arbat and Panfilov bustling with cafes,
shops, street performers, and sculptures, all set against a backdrop of stunning mountains, making it feel safe and pleasant day and night.

FC Kairat Almaty highlight their historic achievement reaching the UEFA Champions League (UCL) league phase in 2025
